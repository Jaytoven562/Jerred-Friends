Donate

Donations are critical to the survival of animal shelters, and every contribution makes a difference in the lives of the animals they serve. Without donations, shelters would not be able to provide the necessary care and support for animals in need, including food, medical attention, and shelter. Unfortunately, many shelters struggle to stay open due to lack of funding, which means that countless animals are left without homes or care. In fact, it's estimated that thousands of animal shelters across the country are forced to close their doors each year due to financial difficulties. Your donation can make a direct impact on the lives of animals in need, helping to ensure that they receive the care and attention they deserve. Whether you're able to contribute a little or a lot, every dollar counts and can help make a difference in the lives of these furry friends.

How this helps us

Veterinary Care

Your donation can go towards providing veterinary care for our animals. Just like humans, animals need regular check-ups and medical attention to stay healthy. Your support can help us cover these costs and ensure that our animals receive the best possible care. Did you know that animal shelters spend an average of $12 billion per year on animal care and services? This includes everything from routine check-ups and vaccinations to surgeries and other necessary medical treatments. We are grateful for any amount you can contribute and appreciate your support in our mission to provide loving homes and medical care for our furry friends.

Dog/Cat Food

Your donation can also go towards providing food for the animals in our care. It takes a lot of food to keep our furry friends well-fed and healthy, and your support can help us cover these costs. Did you know that animal shelters spend an average of $2 billion per year on food for their animals? That's because they have to feed a lot of mouths, and many animals require special diets to meet their unique nutritional needs. By making a donation, you can help us ensure that our animals are well-fed and have everything they need to thrive. We appreciate your support in our mission to provide loving homes and care for our four-legged friends.

Pet Supplies

Donations are crucial for animal shelters to provide basic needs and comfort for the animals in their care. Funds received from donations help shelters purchase essential pet supplies such as beds, toys, kennels, leashes, and more. These items are not only necessary for the well-being of the animals, but they also help create a more homely and comfortable environment for them. Thanks to generous donations from individuals and organizations, shelters can continue to provide the best care possible for animals waiting to be adopted. Every contribution, big or small, can make a significant impact on the lives of these animals.
